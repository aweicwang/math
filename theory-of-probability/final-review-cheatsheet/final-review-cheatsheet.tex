\section{Law of Large Numbers}

\textbf{Definition.} $X_n$ \emph{converges in probability} to $X$ if
                     for any $\varepsilon > 0$,
                     $$\lim_{n \to \infty}\mathbb{P}(|X_n-X| \geq \varepsilon) = 0 $$
                     denoted by $X_n \xrightarrow{p} X $.
